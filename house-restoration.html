Work Background

The Green Haven Project is designed as an innovative housing community focused on eco-friendliness and modern aesthetics.

This initiative seeks to combine sustainable materials with cutting-edge building techniques to create a cozy and smart living environment.

Throughout the entire process, we collaborated closely—performing environmental assessments, utilizing modular design, and incorporating smart technology. Striking a balance between style and energy efficiency was a significant challenge.

The Outcome

We finished a modular home that incorporates eco-friendly materials, superior insulation, and solar-powered roofing.

The design focused on long-term energy efficiency while maintaining architectural harmony and comfort.

The construction reduced environmental impact, increased material durability, and emerged as a highlight in Ecohaus’s upcoming projects.
