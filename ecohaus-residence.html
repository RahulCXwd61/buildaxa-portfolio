ECOHAUS RESIDENCE

Audited architectural planning in detail, refined sustainable design principles thoughtfully, & developed an innovative modular construction framework focused on efficiency, adaptability, and environmental responsibility.

Aug 22, 2025

CLIENT
Ecohaus Group
ROLE
Lead Architect
SERVICE
Architecture Design

Work Background

Ecohaus Residence was envisioned as a future-forward housing project rooted in sustainability and modern design.

The goal was to merge eco-friendly materials with high-performance construction techniques to create a comfortable, smart living environment.

We collaborated from initial planning to final construction. We conducted environmental site analysis, applied modular architecture strategies, and implemented smart home technologies into the design system. The challenge was maintaining elegance while optimizing for energy efficiency.

The Outcome

We delivered a modular residence with sustainable materials, advanced insulation, and solar-integrated roofing systems.

The design ensured long-term energy efficiency while maintaining architectural harmony & comfort.

The construction reduced environmental impact, improved material lifecycle, and became a signature residential showcase for Ecohaus’s future portfolio.
